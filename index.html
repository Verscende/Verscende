What is Verscende ?

The Verscende app works like this, the program helps you make money by helping you create music videos.

It does this by engaging in a multiple brief conversations with you,to learn more about your preferences in music,it then uses this information to determine what kind of 3D animation or effect, would complement the particular songs you like,by detecting the genre/emotional impact of that song(s) your have listened to and combining it with its comparable 3D effect and animation.

The end result is a music video that will surely impress.
